About LeMo

LeMo: Leading the Way in Mobile Usage Control

LeMo is a UK-based company specializing in mobile and web application development. Our innovative app allows you to restrict mobile phone access, blocking social media sites and more, to ensure a focused, distraction-free environment.

Unique Features:

  • QR Code Scanning: Instantly restrict access when scanning a unique QR code.
  • Geotagging Technology: Automatically manage mobile usage based on location.
  • Designed for Educational Bodies: Specifically tailored to support schools and universities, creating an optimal learning experience for students.

Mission Statement

Supporting Global Education, One School at a Time

At LeMo, our mission is to help schools and educational organizations worldwide promote an undistracted, productive environment for students and pupils. By leveraging our innovative mobile restriction tools, we empower institutions to provide a full, uninterrupted learning experience.

How LeMo Works

QR Code Scanning

Simply scan a QR code to instantly block access to distracting sites.

Geotagging Control

Set location-based rules to restrict access when students are within specific areas like classrooms, libraries, or exam halls.

Customizable Settings

Customizable settings that allow for tailored phone restrictions according to time, place, or event.
